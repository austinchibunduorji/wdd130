Purpose

mbark on a thrilling whitewater rafting trip at the Selangor River, claimed to have one of the best rapids in South East Asia! Thanks to the tropical climate in Malaysia, whitewater rafting is an activity that can be enjoyed pretty much all year round. Put on your life jacket and get ready for an adrenaline-pumping rafting adventure with spectacular views of nature along the riverbanks. Don’t worry if it’s your first time, you will be guided by a friendly river guide that will provide paddling commands and remind you of safety measures to ensure a fun journey for all. This rafting trip excursion covers fees for a professional guide (who will conduct a safety briefing and ensure a safe and enjoyable journey for all participants), snacks, a sumptuous lunch, and all necessary safety gear and rafting equipment. Enjoy a hassle-free trip when you book your tour voucher on Klook!
